# Product Backlog (The Velvet Room)

- US1: As a customer, I want to view the private suites and tasting menus online.
- US2: As a customer, I want to submit a reservation with date, time, and pax.
- US3: As a system, I need to store reservation data and room details.
- US4: As an admin, I want to securely log into the dashboard.
- US5: As an admin, I want to view all bookings and manage room availability.
- US6: As a system, I must prevent double-booking on the same time slot.
